Mindshine

We are a team made up of first-year students Isaac Newton and Louis Pasteur from Colégio Pro Mundo. We are dedicated to creating a website that helps promote digital accessibility for people with visual impairments. Our team is made up of enthusiastic and committed members who want to contribute to making the digital world more inclusive for all. We believe that everyone deserves access to information and technology, regardless of their visual ability, and we are working together to make a difference. We look forward to sharing our work with the community and helping to create a more accessible world.
