objective

The ZOOM/LEGO Robotics Tournament is a culminating project of our robotics classes developed throughout the school year with different activities, from more structured proposals to the most open and challenging, which integrate and complement each other to form a learning journey for students to experience.

in a playful way and with purpose, technological concepts and develop the necessary skills to live in an increasingly complex world.

what will it be like?

The event is a great spectacle of technology and innovation, with teams programming, designing and building robots that can solve local and global problems so that we can live in an increasingly sustainable world.
